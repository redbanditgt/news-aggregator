Policy Making Process

1

Agenda Setting

Issues identified and prioritized by policymakers

Example: Beti Bachao Beti Padhao campaign addressing declining child sex ratio
2

Policy Formulation

Crafting policy alternatives and solutions

Example: Designing schemes like MGNREGA for rural employment
3

Adoption

Government approval and legislative passage

Example: Parliamentary debate and bill passing process
4

Implementation

Execution by government agencies and departments

Example: Field-level implementation by civil servants
5

Evaluation

Assessment of policy effectiveness and outcomes

Example: Review of NEP 2020 implementation and results
6

Policy Maintenance

Revision, continuation, or termination of policies

Example: Updating policies based on performance and changing needs
